Welcome To My Notes Management System


This system will provide an easy approach to share the documents for studying purpose. Multiple students can work simultaneously on the system. It will be easy for the teachers to circulate the notes to each and every student. The system will be used by students and teachers in universities and schools. The objective is to provide better facility to the students and teachers to bring out the easy circulation of documents within healthy environment. It will reduce the manual paper work,reduce the sharing and distributing time.


The scope of this system is study @ anytime,anywhere.
